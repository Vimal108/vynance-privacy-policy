Privacy Policy for Vynance

Last updated: January 2026

Vynance (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Vynance mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address, encrypted authentication credentials, and a unique user ID.

Financial Data: Budgets, transactions, categories, and notes you choose to enter. Stored locally and synced securely to the cloud.

Usage Data: Anonymous usage and diagnostic data for performance and stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide app functionality, authenticate users, sync data, improve performance, and communicate service updates. We do not sell or share data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device and securely in the cloud for backup and synchronization purposes only.

4. Third-Party Services

Vynance uses trusted third-party services for authentication, cloud storage, analytics, and crash reporting.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time. Cloud data will be permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Our app collects only the data necessary to provide you with a seamless financial tracking experience, such as account names, balances, and transaction data. If you would like us to delete your data, you can submit a request by emailing us at: vyrewebsites@gmail.com
